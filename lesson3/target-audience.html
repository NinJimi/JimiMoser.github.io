Target Audience

Locals

The first audience is for locals. It is for them to be able to look at the site to get a fast glance at the weather and to learn what would be appropriate to wear that day.

Kids

I can see kids using this site as well. Most kids have a hard time deciding what to wear. Also they don't understand weather and how it works as well. My son for example wants to wear only his pajamas all the time.
